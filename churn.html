// HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. Runs every morning.

Set it once. The bot watches your customers from then on.

01
Daily 8am scan
The bot reads your full customer database every morning. Calculates days since last purchase, compares to each customer's typical frequency, weights by loyalty tier.
02
Risk-tier each customer
Critical / At Risk / Watching — based on a churn ratio that combines silence duration, normal cadence, and lifetime value. VIPs get tighter thresholds.
03
Run win-back automatically
A 3-stage email sequence kicks off — gentle, then firm, then final. If they buy at any point, the sequence stops. You get a Monday brief showing who's where.
// THE SCORING

Four signals. One churn ratio.

Loyal customers get more grace before they're flagged. Casual customers get flagged faster. Tiered intelligence — not blanket rules.

R
Recency
How long since their last purchase, compared to their normal cadence. Not just "how long" — but "how long compared to usual".
F
Frequency
How often they used to buy. A customer who bought weekly going silent for 30 days is more urgent than a quarterly buyer at the same gap.
T
Tier weighting
VIP and Gold customers get tighter churn thresholds. Their silence costs more, so they're flagged earlier.
V
Value at stake
Lifetime value sets the priority. Two equally-silent customers — the one worth R30K gets attention before the one worth R3K.
// THE WIN-BACK SEQUENCE

Three emails. One escalating tone.

Each customer gets exactly the right pressure for their tier. The sequence stops the moment they buy.

01
DAY 0
Gentle
A warm "we miss you" note. No pressure, no offer. Just acknowledgment that they've been a great customer, and you're thinking of them.
02
DAY +5
Firm
A direct message with something concrete — a personalised offer or invitation back. References their tier and last purchase. Slightly more urgency.
03
DAY +12
Final
The last automated touch. Honest about the gap, generous with the offer, but clear this is the final attempt. After this, owner takes over manually.
