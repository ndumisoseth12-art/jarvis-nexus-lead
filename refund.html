0230-day money-back guarantee.

Plain English: If our bots don't deliver in your first 30 days, we refund the setup fee and the first month's recurring amount in full. No forms, no friction.

For any new subscription, you may request a full refund of:

provided that the request is made within 30 calendar days of your initial payment date.

This guarantee applies once per business and to the first subscription only. It does not apply to renewals, additional bots added later, or a second subscription started after a previous cancellation.

04Refund processing & timing.

All refunds are processed through PayFast, our payment processor, back to the original payment method used for the purchase. We do not refund to a different card, bank account, or third party.

Standard refund timeline:

  • Within 2 business days — we acknowledge your request
  • Within 5 business days — we initiate the refund through PayFast
  • Within 10 business days — the refund settles back to your card

Bank settlement times vary by card issuer. PayFast typically returns funds within 3–7 business days once initiated, but some issuers can take up to 10 business days. If you have not seen the refund after 10 business days from our confirmation, contact us and we will provide the PayFast transaction reference.

Refunds are issued in the original currency (South African Rand, ZAR). We do not refund for any difference in exchange rate, foreign card conversion fees, or bank charges levied by your card issuer.

06Setup fees after 30 days.

The setup fee covers custom bot configuration, brand voice tuning, integration with your sheets and calendar, and the onboarding session. This is work delivered in the first 7–14 days of your subscription.

If you cancel within the 30-day guarantee window, the setup fee is fully refundable under §02.

If you cancel after the 30-day window, the setup fee is non-refundable because the work has been delivered and the bots are running. This is consistent with the Consumer Protection Act §17 (cooling-off does not apply to services already substantially performed at the consumer's request).

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain full access to all bots and support until that date.

If you believe a charge is incorrect or unauthorised, please contact us first at ndumisoseth12@gmail.com before lodging a chargeback. We will investigate and respond within 5 business days. Most billing disputes can be resolved directly and more quickly than the chargeback process.

If a chargeback is upheld in your favour, no further action is required. If the chargeback is reversed, the original charge stands and your subscription resumes.
