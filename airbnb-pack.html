// HOSTS ASK ME

The questions every Airbnb host has before signing up.

Honest answers. Tap any question.

How many listings does this work for? +

The Airbnb Pack is sized for hosts with 1–5 active listings. The 500-event monthly cap fits comfortably in that range — each new review, drafted reply, and recovered payment counts as one event. If you have 6+ listings or a serious property-management operation, look at the Operator or Command tier where the events scale up and you get custom AI voice per listing.

What if my listings are also on Booking.com or Vrbo? +

Review Pro handles Airbnb, Booking.com and Google reviews out of the box. Vrbo and HelloPeter integrations are on the roadmap — currently we route those manually for early clients (you forward the review notification, the bot drafts the reply, you post it). Be honest about your platform mix on the demo call and I'll tell you what's automated vs manual today.

Will the bot reply to negative reviews on its own? +

No. Any review 3★ or below gets flagged for your personal approval. The bot still drafts a thoughtful, de-escalating response — but it never posts a sensitive reply automatically. Your reputation is too important to delegate to AI without a human glance.

What about international guests who don't speak English well? +

The bot reads the review in whatever language the guest wrote it (Dutch, German, French, Mandarin — the LLM handles it natively) and drafts your reply in English by default. If you want replies in the guest's language, that's a paid add-on we can configure during onboarding. Most SA hosts reply in English and guests are happy.

Can it actually recover damage claims that are weeks old? +

Yes — Revenue Recovery handles claims of any age. Average recovery rate is around 70% on claims under 30 days old, dropping to ~40% after 60 days. The bot won't perform miracles on a claim from 2024, but for anything in the last few months, it's worth running. You import the open claims into a sheet during onboarding and the chase ladder kicks off the next morning.

What if my guests prefer WhatsApp for communication? +

Right now Revenue Recovery chases via email + SMS. The WhatsApp Lead Catcher (our WhatsApp Business integration) launches Phase 3 — we're waiting on the SA WhatsApp Business account approval from Meta. Until then, email + SMS reaches 95% of guests. When WhatsApp goes live, your account gets upgraded at no extra cost.

I'm a property manager with 30+ listings — does this scale? +

The Airbnb Pack is sized for individual hosts. For property managers running 10+ listings, the Operator tier (R3,990/mo, 5,000 events, custom AI voice per listing) is the right fit. For 30+ listings or agency-style operations, the Command tier (R9,990/mo, unlimited, quarterly strategy session) is built for that scale. Book a call and I'll size you correctly — no point overpaying or hitting caps mid-month.

What if I cancel — do I lose my data? +

No. Your reviews, drafted replies, damage claim history and guest log all live in your own Google Sheets, in your own Google account. The bots simply turn off. You keep everything. Month-to-month. 30-day money-back on your first month, no questions asked. For Airbnb Pack clients you also get my direct WhatsApp — +27 67 099 4163 — if anything urgent comes up between briefs.
