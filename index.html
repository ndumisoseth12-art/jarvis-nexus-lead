// HONEST ANSWERS

The questions everyone asks me before they sign up.

No marketing fluff. Tap any question.

What exactly is a "customer event"? +

One thing the bots do for you. Replying to one review = 1 event. Chasing one failed payment = 1 event. Sending one no-show rescue offer = 1 event. So 500/month on Starter ≈ 16 events a day — plenty for a salon, dentist, café or solo Airbnb host. If you hit the cap mid-month I'll tell you, never silently bill you for overage.

Do I need any technical skills? +

None. I set everything up. You give me your Gmail, your Google Sheet, your Cal.com link (or I make you one) — I wire the bots into them in a 60-minute onboarding call. After that you just answer your phone when a hot lead comes in. If you can read an email, you can run Jarvis Nexus.

Where does my data live? Can I take it with me? +

In your own Google Sheets, in your own Google account. Always. You own every customer record, every reply, every transcript. If you ever leave Jarvis Nexus, your data stays exactly where it was — I just turn the bots off. Nothing locked behind a dashboard you can't access.

What if I cancel — do I lose everything? +

No. Your sheets and customer data stay with you (it's already in your Google account). The bots simply stop running. If you re-subscribe later, I switch them back on. Month-to-month. Cancel any time. 30-day money-back on your first month, no questions asked.

What about WhatsApp? My customers only use WhatsApp. +

Right now 9 bots run on email + Telegram. WhatsApp Lead Catcher (Bot 1) launches in Phase 3 once my SA WhatsApp Business account is approved by Meta. Until then, for early clients, I route WhatsApp enquiries manually — you forward them to me, I queue them through. Honest about it, no smoke and mirrors.

You're one person — what if something breaks at midnight? +

Every bot has an error workflow that pings me on Telegram the second anything fails. I'm in Joburg — same timezone as you. The system is built so the bots keep replying even when I'm asleep, and failures wait in my inbox for the morning. For Operator and Command clients you also get my direct WhatsApp for anything urgent.
