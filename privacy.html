01Who we are.

"Jarvis Nexus" (the "Service", "we", "us", "our") is a sole-proprietorship business operated by Seth Mokone, based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We build, deploy and operate AI automation workflows for small and medium South African businesses ("customers", "you").

For the purposes of the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA), Jarvis Nexus is the Responsible Party for personal information collected through this website and the Operator for personal information processed on behalf of our subscribed customers (the SMBs whose bots we run).

02What we collect.

From visitors and prospects

When you fill in the AI Router form, the demo-booking form, or contact us via WhatsApp/email, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • The message content you submit (used to route you to the right bot)
  • Technical metadata: your IP address, the page you came from, the time of submission

From subscribed customers

When you subscribe to a Starter, Operator or Command plan:

  • Account info — same as above, plus business name and the bots you've enabled
  • Billing info — handled by PayFast (Pty) Ltd. We never see your full card number. We receive only: payment status, payment ID, billing token, and amount
  • Configuration data — the spreadsheet IDs, Gmail addresses, Telegram chat IDs and other settings you provide so the bots can act on your behalf

From your customers (data we process on your behalf)

Once your subscription is active, our bots process data you route to them — the personal information of your customers. Examples:

  • Lead names, email addresses, phone numbers (Lead Catcher, Reactivation)
  • Appointment details (No-Show Rescue)
  • Review content and ratings (Reputation Shield, Review Pro)
  • Support messages and ticket history (Smart Support Triage)
  • Purchase history (Lifetime & Next Action, Revenue Recovery)

This data is processed strictly to execute the workflow you've configured. We act as the Operator under POPIA — you remain the Responsible Party for your customers' data. We don't use your customers' data for our own purposes, ever.

05Cross-border transfers.

Some of our sub-processors are located outside South Africa (mostly in the United States and the European Union). When your personal information is transferred to a country outside the Republic, we rely on POPIA section 72, namely:

  • Your consent — by using the Service, you consent to this transfer where applicable; and/or
  • Contractual safeguards — each sub-processor is bound by their own data protection terms (most have GDPR-compliant Data Processing Addenda we have accepted on your behalf) that provide a level of protection substantially similar to POPIA

You can withdraw consent for cross-border transfers at any time — see section Your rights — but doing so will prevent us from delivering the Service to you.

06How long we keep it.

  • Prospect data (Router form submissions, contact requests) — kept for 12 months from your last interaction with us, then deleted unless you've become a customer
  • Active customer data — kept for the duration of your subscription, plus 30 days after cancellation in case you re-subscribe
  • Billing records — kept for 5 years from the date of the transaction, to comply with the Income Tax Act and the Companies Act record-keeping requirements
  • Customer-of-customer data (your end users) — retained only while needed to execute the workflow, then automatically purged according to the retention period you configure (typical defaults: 90 days for transactional logs, indefinite for opt-in CRM data)
  • Email correspondence — kept for as long as needed to provide support and resolve disputes

07Your rights under POPIA.

You have the following rights, free of charge, exercisable by emailing ndumisoseth12@gmail.com:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction — ask us to fix anything inaccurate or out-of-date
  • Right to deletion — ask us to delete your personal information (subject to legal retention obligations above)
  • Right to object — object to specific processing activities
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, with no penalty (but it may prevent us from delivering the Service)
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with the South African Information Regulator at inforegulator.org.za

We will respond to all valid requests within a reasonable time, and in any event no later than 30 days from receipt.

08How we protect your data.

We take the security of personal information seriously. Our safeguards include:

  • Encryption in transit — HTTPS/TLS on every page; HSTS preload; secure WebSocket connections to all sub-processors
  • Encryption at rest — provided by Google Workspace (AES-256), Railway, and Vercel infrastructure
  • Access controls — OAuth 2.0 to Gmail and Sheets; secret credentials stored as environment variables, never committed to source control
  • Signature verification — all PayFast notifications are MD5-signed and verified before any action is taken
  • Audit trail — every payment and every critical bot action is logged with timestamps, user identifiers and status
  • Content Security Policy — strict CSP, HSTS, X-Content-Type-Options, Permissions-Policy and Referrer-Policy headers on every page
  • Minimised inline auth — operator-only pages restrict access via passphrase gates; full role-based access control is on our roadmap

If we ever discover a security breach that compromises your personal information, we will notify you and the Information Regulator as required by POPIA section 22, as soon as reasonably possible.

09Cookies & analytics.

At present, this website does not set any tracking cookies. We use only essential storage (browser localStorage) for operator-only pages to remember that an authorised user has unlocked the dashboard.

If we add web analytics in future (likely Vercel Analytics or Plausible Analytics, both privacy-friendly and POPIA-compliant), we will update this policy and obtain consent where required.
