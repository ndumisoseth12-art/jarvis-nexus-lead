// THE TWO PATHS

One private answer. Two completely different next steps.

Most "review request" bots ask publicly first and pray. Reputation Shield asks privately, decides intelligently, and only sends customers to Google when they're already happy.

4 OR 5 STARS
Promote → public review
A short, warm follow-up asking them to share publicly — with your Google review link front and centre. No friction, no awkwardness.
  • Thanks them for the rating
  • Asks for a public Google review
  • One-click CTA to your review page
  • Sheet logged as "Public review sent"
1, 2 OR 3 STARS
Recover → private follow-up
Heartfelt service-recovery email goes to the customer instead. Acknowledges the let-down, invites a reply. Owner gets a Telegram alert immediately so a phone call can follow.
  • Personal apology, no defensiveness
  • Invites direct reply for resolution
  • Telegram pings owner instantly
  • Public review ask is blocked entirely
// HOW IT WORKS

Three moves. Reputation protected.

No reviews managed manually. No awkward "please give us 5 stars" emails. Just clean, intelligent routing.

01
Trigger after a sale
Hit the webhook (or trigger from your operator dashboard) once a job is done. Bot waits a few days for the experience to settle, then sends the private rating ask.
02
Customer rates privately
One-click stars in their email. Their sentiment is captured before any public-facing ask ever happens. They feel heard, you stay in control.
03
Bot routes intelligently
4+ stars → public Google review ask. 3 or below → private service-recovery email + Telegram alert to you. Every check tracked in your sheet, status updated automatically.
