// SALON OWNERS ASK ME

The questions every salon owner has before signing up.

Honest answers. Tap any question.

My salon is small — only 1 or 2 chairs. Is this for me? +

Yes — actually, the smaller you are, the more this matters. A 1-chair salon can't afford to lose even 1 Saturday booking. The Salon Pack is priced specifically so that recovering just one no-show per month pays for the whole bot suite. If you're doing 30+ bookings a month and not tracking your no-show rate, you're definitely the right fit.

Do my clients need to install an app or sign up to anything? +

Nothing. Your clients receive normal emails. They tap a link, pick a new time, done. No new apps. No login. No "create an account" friction. If your gogo can book online, she can use the rebook link.

Will the bot message my clients without me knowing? +

No-Show Rescue sends the rescue offer automatically — that's the whole point, speed matters. But every other message gets your approval first. Drafted Google review replies wait in your inbox until you tap "send." You're always in the loop, never surprised.

What if my clients only use WhatsApp? +

Real answer: right now the bots send via email only. WhatsApp Lead Catcher (our WhatsApp Business integration) launches Phase 3 — we're waiting on the SA WhatsApp Business account approval from Meta. Until then, email covers most of your regular clients who already check inbox daily for bookings. When WhatsApp is live, you'll get upgraded at no extra cost.

Can I see what messages are being sent to my clients? +

Every single one. The bots log every action into your own Google Sheet — which client, what was sent, when, and whether they rebooked. You can scroll through and audit any message at any time. You own the data, always.

What about my regulars who I know personally? +

You can mark VIP clients in your sheet — the bot will skip them for No-Show Rescue and ping you on Telegram instead ("Thandi missed her 11:00 — do you want to call her yourself?"). For your regulars, the bot becomes an assistant, not a salesperson. You decide who gets the human touch.

What happens if I cancel? +

You stop being charged. Your sheets and customer data stay in your Google account — they were always yours. The bots simply turn off. If you re-subscribe later, I switch them back on. Month-to-month. Cancel from your inbox. 30-day money-back on your first month, no questions asked.

You're one person — what if something breaks at 8pm on a Friday? +

Every bot has an error workflow that pings me on Telegram the second anything fails. I'm in Joburg — same timezone as you. The system is built so the bots keep running even when I'm asleep, and any failures wait in my inbox for the morning.
