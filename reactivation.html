// HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. One trigger.

You decide the offer and the silence threshold. The bot does the rest.

01
Trigger a campaign
From your operator dashboard (or one webhook hit), set the incentive and the silence threshold — 30 days, 60, 90, whatever fits your business cycle.
02
Bot pulls the silent list
Reads your Lifetime database, filters to customers who haven't bought past your threshold, sorts by tier and value. Highest-priority customers get drafted first.
03
AI drafts + sends one by one
Each email is personally written using their name, tier, last product, and your incentive. Sent with an 8-second pause between sends to keep deliverability clean. Logged automatically.
// THE FEATURES

List-aware. Tier-aware. Owner-aware.

Built so a single campaign feels like 47 personal hand-written emails — without the 47 hours that would actually take.

L
List from Lifetime
Pulls directly from your Lifetime & Next Action database. No manual export. No copy-paste. The list that powers it is always current.
P
Personal AI emails
Each email written individually using their name, tier, last purchase, and your incentive. SA English. Warm, not desperate. Never feels templated.
I
Incentive built-in
You set the offer per campaign — 15% off, free delivery, a free add-on. The AI weaves it naturally into each email and surfaces it in a clean visual block.
T
Tracked & recovered
Every send is logged to a dedicated Reactivation tab. When a silent customer buys again, the row is marked recovered — so you know what worked.
