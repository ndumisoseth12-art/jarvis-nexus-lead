Lead Triage.

B11 · REVIEW PRO
Review Pro.

  • A draft reply waiting in Telegram before you've even opened Google
  • Matches your tone — straight-talking, no corporate fluff
  • Flags 1-2★ reviews privately before they go public — so you can call the customer first if needed
  • Your star average climbs week-over-week without you typing a thing

// TRADESMEN ASK ME

The questions every tradesman has before signing up.

Honest answers. Tap any question.

I'm a one-man-band — I don't have a website. Does this still work? +

Yes — and it's actually easier when you don't have a fancy website yet. As part of the R2,500 setup I build you a simple one-page quote form (your name, your number, your areas, your trade) and host it for you. Customers go to yourtrade.co.za (or wherever) and fill in the basics — the bot takes it from there. You don't need WordPress, Wix, or any of that. If you already have a website, I just plug the form into your existing one.

I can't quote properly without seeing the job. Won't the bot give a wrong price? +

You're right — and the bot is built knowing that. It never quotes the final job. It only quotes the callout fee (R450, R650, whatever you tell me on the onboarding call) and says "I'll quote the rest once I see it on site." That covers your time to drive out and protects you from doing free quotes. The customer knows what they're paying to get you there — and that's usually the biggest blocker to them confirming.

Will the bot message customers without me knowing? +

Lead Triage sends the auto-reply automatically — that's the whole point, speed wins the job. But every other message gets your approval first. Drafted Google review replies wait in your inbox until you tap "send." And every auto-quote that goes out gets a Telegram copy to you, so you can call the customer in 30 seconds if something looks off.

What if my customers only use WhatsApp? +

Real answer: right now the bots send via email only. WhatsApp Lead Catcher (our WhatsApp Business integration) launches Phase 3 — we're waiting on the SA WhatsApp Business account approval from Meta. Until then, the quote form captures their email + number, the bot replies via email AND your Telegram gets the alert with their phone number ready to call — so you can WhatsApp them yourself in 10 seconds if you prefer. When WhatsApp Business is live, you'll get upgraded at no extra cost.

Can I see what's being sent on my behalf? +

Every single message. The bots log every enquiry, every quote, every review reply into your own Google Sheet — who, what, when, whether they booked. You can scroll through and audit anything at any time. You own the data, always.

What if I get a bad review? Won't the bot say something stupid? +

For 1-2 star reviews, the bot never posts automatically. It drafts a calm, professional reply and pings you on Telegram with a heads-up: "Bad review from X — drafted a reply, but you should probably call them first." You can post the draft, edit it, or scrap it and call the customer directly. The bot protects you from the worst mistake — replying angry while you're still hot.

What happens if I cancel? +

You stop being charged. Your sheets and customer data stay in your Google account — they were always yours. The bots simply turn off. The quote form keeps working as a normal form (just without the auto-reply). If you re-subscribe later, I switch them back on. Month-to-month. Cancel from your inbox. 30-day money-back on your first month, no questions asked.

You're one person — what if something breaks at 8pm on a Friday? +

Every bot has an error workflow that pings me on Telegram the second anything fails. I'm in Joburg — same timezone as you. The system is built so the bots keep running even when I'm asleep, and any failures wait in my inbox for the morning. For Trade Pack clients, you also get my direct WhatsApp for anything urgent — +27 67 099 4163.
