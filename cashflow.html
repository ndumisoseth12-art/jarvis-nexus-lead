// HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. Runs itself every Friday.

No new admin. It reads the invoice data you already keep.

01
Reads your invoices
Every Friday at 6am the bot pulls due dates and amounts from your invoice sheet and your Paystack receipts. Nothing for you to update — it uses what’s already there.
02
Projects 7 / 14 / 30 days
It sums what’s expected in each window, then checks concentration — if a single debtor is more than half your fortnight, it gets flagged as a risk.
03
Sends the Friday brief
One email before the weekend: the three totals, the concentration flag, and one recommended action — ranked by which chase protects the most cash.
