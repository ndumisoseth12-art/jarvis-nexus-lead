// WHAT'S IN THE PACK

Three bots. One job each. All running for you 24/7.

No software to install. No new app for you or your clients. The bots plug into the tools you already use — Gmail, Google Sheets, your calendar — and work quietly in the background while you train.

1
B3 · NO-SHOW RESCUE
No-Show Rescue.

When a client skips a session, the bot sends a friendly rescheduling offer within 10 minutes — before guilt sets in and they stop replying. One tap and they're rebooked into the next open slot.

  • Triggers the moment your calendar marks a session as missed — you don't have to remember
  • Client rebooks with one tap — no awkward WhatsApp dance
  • Three-touch sequence built in: nudge day-of, friendly check day-3, soft last-chance day-7
  • Rebook rate averages 30–40% within 24 hours
2
B5 · REACTIVATION
Reactivation.

Clients who go quiet for 2–3 weeks usually don't come back unless you reach out. The bot watches for silent clients and sends a warm, no-guilt re-engage email that references their specific goal and last session — not a generic "we miss you".

  • Catches dropout clients in the 14–21 day silent window (the only window that still converts)
  • References the specific goal + last session — credibility through specifics, not "Hi {firstName}"
  • VIP clients with high lifetime value get a personal Telegram ping to YOU instead — you decide who needs the human call
  • Typical recovery: 1 in 4 silent clients re-books from a single re-engage
3
B8 · COACH BRIEF
Coach Brief.

Every morning at 6am, one email lands in your inbox before the first client. Yesterday's saved sessions. Today's at-risk clients (missed-2-in-a-row, late-cancel patterns). Who needs a personal nudge from you. No dashboards.

  • Wakes up before your 5am client and tells you who's on the edge of quitting
  • Yesterday's Rand saved · today's at-risk clients · one screen
  • Flags the pattern most trainers miss: 2 missed in a row = 70% they don't come back
  • 2-minute read on your way to the gym · then you're done

// TRAINERS ASK ME

The questions every coach has before signing up.

Honest answers. Tap any question.

I'm a solo trainer with maybe 15 clients. Is this overkill? +

It's actually more important when you're solo. With 15 clients, losing 2 to silent dropout in a single month is 13% of your income gone — and you usually don't notice until rent week. The Trainer Pack is priced specifically so that saving just one client per month from quietly quitting pays for the whole bot suite. If you've ever opened your calendar on a Sunday night and realised someone you used to train every Tuesday hasn't booked in 3 weeks, you're the right fit.

Do my clients need to install an app or sign up to anything? +

Nothing. Your clients receive normal emails. They tap a link, pick a new time, done. No new apps. No login. No "create an account" friction. If your client can book a flight online, they can use the rebook link.

Won't the rescue email feel cold or robotic? Training is personal. +

Fair worry — and the answer is in how it's written. The bot references the specific session they missed and their actual goal (strength block · fat loss · rehab) and uses your tone of voice from the onboarding call. The reactivation email goes further — it mentions the specific last thing they were working on ("your bench was finally moving"). It reads like a thoughtful trainer who noticed, not a CRM blast. Real example outputs are in the section above.

What if my clients only use WhatsApp? +

Real answer: right now the bots send via email only. WhatsApp Lead Catcher (our WhatsApp Business integration) launches Phase 3 — we're waiting on the SA WhatsApp Business account approval from Meta. Until then, email covers most regular clients who already check inbox daily — and the rescue / re-engage emails read so well on a phone screen that the channel matters less than you'd think. When WhatsApp is live, you'll get upgraded at no extra cost.

Can I see what messages are being sent to my clients? +

Every single one. The bots log every action into your own Google Sheet — which client, what was sent, when, and whether they rebooked. You can scroll through and audit any message at any time. You own the data, always.

What about my long-term clients I know personally? +

You can mark VIP clients in your sheet — the bot will skip them for the automated emails and ping you on Telegram instead ("Lebo missed her 06:00 — do you want to call her yourself?"). For your long-haul clients, the bot becomes an early-warning system, not a salesperson. You decide who gets the human touch.

What happens if I cancel? +

You stop being charged. Your sheets and client data stay in your Google account — they were always yours. The bots simply turn off. If you re-subscribe later, I switch them back on. Month-to-month. Cancel from your inbox. 30-day money-back on your first month, no questions asked.

You're one person — what if something breaks at 5am on a Monday? +

Every bot has an error workflow that pings me on Telegram the second anything fails. I'm in Joburg — same timezone as you. The system is built so the bots keep running even when I'm asleep, and any failures wait in my inbox for the morning.
