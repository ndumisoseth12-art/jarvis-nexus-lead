Going live with Paystack.

The day your Paystack approval email lands, open this page and go top to bottom. It takes about 30 minutes, and you barely touch any code — almost all of it is clicking around two dashboards (Paystack and Railway). There's exactly one tiny website change at the end. Take your time, tick each box, and don't skip the test payment.

// The steps

Eight steps, in order

1Get your real keys🟣 Paystack

A secret key is like a master password that lets your system actually charge cards. Paystack gives you a test one (fake money) and a live one (real money). Right now you're on test — you're switching to live.

  1. Open dashboard.paystack.com.
  2. Top-right, flip the Test / Live toggle to Live.
  3. Left menu → Settings → API Keys & Webhooks.
  4. Copy the Live Secret Key (it starts with sk_live_). Paste it into your phone notes for a minute — you'll need it in Step 3.
⚠ Never share that secret key. Anyone who has it can charge cards through your account. Don't paste it into a chat, screenshot, or anywhere public.

// If something goes wrong

The 3 things that usually break

"I paid but no Telegram / no sheet row."
The payment didn't reach your bots, or a bot errored. Open n8n → Executions. No execution at all → in Paystack, Settings → Webhooks → Recent events → click the failed one → Resend. An execution with a red ✕ on "Verify Signature" → your live secret key in Railway (Step 3) is wrong or still the test one — fix it and redeploy.
"The Subscribe button gives a blank white page."
The subscribe bot errored before showing checkout — almost always the PAYSTACK_SECRET_KEY in Railway isn't set or is wrong. Re-check Step 3.
"Welcome email says the customer's name is 'New Customer'."
Paystack didn't capture their name at checkout. That's fine — it's the safe fallback. To collect names, turn on name fields in Paystack → Settings → Preferences → Checkout fields.
Still stuck? Paystack support: support@paystack.com (replies within a day). Or message me — but this page has the 95% case.
