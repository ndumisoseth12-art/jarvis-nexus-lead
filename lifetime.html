// HOW IT WORKS

Log it once. Forever ranked.

One webhook hit per purchase. The bot does the rest — for the lifetime of every customer.

01
Log every purchase
A simple webhook call when a customer buys. The bot updates lifetime value, recalculates the tier, tracks recent spend trajectory, fires a celebration email if a milestone or tier upgrade was hit.
02
Monday Top 5
Every Monday at 8am: an email + Telegram showing the five customers most worth acting on this week — each with a specific suggestion (upsell, win-back, referral ask, tier-up nudge).
03
Monthly LTV report
First of every month: a styled report showing tier breakdown, total + average LTV, top customer of the month, and an AI-strategist note on what to focus on next month.
// THE FEATURES

Smart. Tier-aware. Trajectory-aware.

Not generic loyalty rewards. Real per-customer intelligence that turns customer data into next actions.

R
RFV scoring
Recency × Frequency × Value, weighted toward Value. Not just "who spends most" — but "who's most worth acting on this week".
T
Tier-aware actions
A VIP gone silent gets a different recommendation than a Bronze on the rise. The next-action engine knows the difference.
M
Milestone celebrations
5th, 10th, 25th, 50th, 100th purchase — a personal AI-drafted thank-you email. Tier upgrades trigger one too. Customers feel seen.
↗
Trajectory detection
Tracks the last 3 purchase amounts. Trending up = upsell signal. Trending down = check-in signal. Stable = leave alone.
