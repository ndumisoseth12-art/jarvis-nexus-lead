// THE SCORING

Four signals. One number.

The lead score isn't magic — it's a weighted read of the four signals that actually predict whether a lead converts.

B
Budget signal
Can they actually pay? Reads stated revenue, budget range, and language cues to estimate spending power.
U
Urgency & timeline
Are they buying now, or browsing? Detects timeline keywords and frustration markers that signal real urgency.
A
Decision authority
Are you talking to the buyer or a gatekeeper? The bot reads role, language and ownership cues to decide.
F
Fit & Need
Does what they want match what you sell? The bot scores how well their stated problem aligns with your offer.
