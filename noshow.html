// HOW IT WORKS

Book it once. Bot handles the rest.

Three moments. Zero owner intervention until something goes wrong.

01
Log the booking
Submit booking details (name, email, service, appointment time) — through this form, your booking platform, or a Telegram message. Bot logs it and schedules a reminder one hour before the slot.
02
Friendly reminder fires
Sixty minutes before the appointment, the customer gets a short, branded reminder email. If their plans changed, they're nudged to let you know — protecting your slot.
03
No-show? Warm rescue.
If the customer doesn't show, mark them no-show with one webhook hit (or button click). Within minutes, AI drafts a warm rebook invite, sends it from your address, and Telegram-pings you so you know.
